Reminders applications were not designed for students.

They remind you when you when your homework is due - not when you should work on it.

Some people thought typing in more fields made a homework planner easier to use.

They

Were

Wrong.

PlanHW is different.

Instead of features for people with full time jobs, PlanHW is a planner for students, like you!

Here's a few examples...

Smart Fields

PlanHW is smart, and knows what you mean when LA Paper due {{timeFull}} is typed. You'll see that PlanHW knows {{timeFull}} is {{timeFromNow}}, and LA is your Language Arts class taught by {{teacher}}, so you can actually pass in your passing period.

Helpful Reminders

Most current reminder systems remind you... when your homework is due. "IF YOU DON'T HAVE YOUR HOMEWORK YET, SOCIETY HATES YOU," is not the message you need when you want into class without homework. PlanHW tells you when you should work on homework, not when it's due.

Simple

With a goal of quick and simple setup, anyone can use PlanHW with only a few minutes to sign up and create your account. Link PlanHW with your existing calender or have it learn when you do your homework. You don't even have to choose when to be reminded, PlanHW does that for you!
